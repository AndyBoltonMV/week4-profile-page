Andy Bolton - Software Developer & Teacher

There are very few jobs that I haven't put my hand to since leaving school in 2004. I've been a Kitchen Fitter, Cleaner, F2F Salesperson, Sales Assistant, Healthcare Assistant, Warehouse Manager and an Operations Manager. Most of those jobs provided me with one thing, a disdain for Monday's (Healthcare Assistant being the outlier).

I'm now a Coach helping people move into the Software Development Industry. Having come into this skill set the same way as my students, passing my experience on as both a professional and an ex-student has eliminated my hatred of Monday's. I never feel like I'm working, almost like I get to share my hobby with people every day.
